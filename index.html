About Us

Who we are

Kouper Lubricants Private Limited is one of the leading speciality lubricant manufacturing company in South India having its facility located at Bangalore. The plant has been setup in the year 2017 and is involved in the manufacturing of specialty Lubricants with production capacity of 2160TPA. The facility has been catering to the Indian Market and its subcontinents whilst serving the international markets.

Kouper Lubricants India Pvt limited has a strong commitment towards the Ecology and Environment system; we comply with all the necessary environmental regulations and standards while committed to responsible sourcing best in its Quality, Manufacturing excellence, Customer centricity and Delivery performance.
